MarketLens AI takes any company profile from YC, Wellfound, or LinkedIn and generates a full marketing intelligence brief in seconds — ICP hypothesis, messaging gaps, positioning risks, content angles, and a single recommended next move.
Built for B2B marketers and GTM teams who need fast, opinionated research without the manual work.

What it does
Paste 1–3 company profiles from any source into the tool, set your target persona, and run the analysis. You get:

Company snapshot — what they do, who they serve, what makes them interesting
ICP hypothesis — primary buyer, secondary persona, and who it's clearly not for
Messaging gaps — what's missing or weak, ranked by severity
Positioning risks — where they could be confused with competitors or lose deals on messaging alone
5 content angles — ready to test, tied to the actual product and ICP pain
The one thing — the single most important marketing move in the next 30 days

Run a combined analysis across multiple profiles, or analyse each source individually.

How to use it

Get a free Groq API key at console.groq.com — no credit card needed
Open the tool in your browser
Paste your Groq API key into the key field
Paste a company profile from YC, Wellfound, LinkedIn, or anywhere
Set your target persona (optional but improves output)
Hit Run

Why I built this
Doing company research for GTM work meant repeating the same manual analysis every time — who's the real buyer, what's the messaging missing, where are the positioning risks. I built this to automate that first-pass intelligence so I could spend time on strategy instead of synthesis.

Local use
No install needed. Just download index.html and open it in any browser.
